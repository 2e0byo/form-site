End of module feedback

At the end of the module I would really appreciate your feedback (both positive and negative) on how things have gone this year.

Think back to the beginning of the course. How does where you are now compare to where you were then: do you find it easier/harder to think/talk about these things? Do you feel like you have more of a grasp of what’s going on, or do the problems seem larger? Do you feel more confident in your understanding, or does it all seem a chaotic muddle? Can you think of anything which has contributed to being this way?

Can you think of an example of something which has and hasn’t worked well? Does anything particularly memorable stand out?

Is there anything you would have liked covered (or covered in more depth) which was not?

Can you think of anything about the presentation of material (slides, whiteboard, texts) which has worked well? And/or which could be improved?

Has this course changed how you approach any of the issues we’ve covered/theology in general? Can you think of an example of how if so?

Is there anything else you would like to add?
